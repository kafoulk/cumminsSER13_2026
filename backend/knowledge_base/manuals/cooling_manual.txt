Cooling System Field Guide (Synthetic)

For recurring over-temperature faults under load, verify coolant concentration, inspect radiator fin blockage, and confirm fan clutch lock-up during commanded high temperature events.

If coolant odor is present with pressure loss, inspect hose clamps, pump weep hole, and thermostat housing seam before replacing sensors.

After repair, perform a controlled warm-up and loaded verification run while recording coolant temp trend and derate counters.
